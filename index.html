About Us

Takcake is a platform where you can find and share the perfect dessert recipe with your choice of ingredients, occasions, and special needs.

Takcake provides a platform for people to find the dessert recipes by selecting the ingredients they have, by choosing what the occasion is for the serving, and by having some special needs to bake various kinds of desserts. People are able to find the recipe they wish or they can bake. Users can not only learn the recipe, but they can also share their recipes on the site. It is a platform perfect for people who are passionate about baking.
